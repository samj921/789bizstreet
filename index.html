789 Biz Street, LLC

Up late with you...

Why is the tagline “Up Late with You?” Because the founder and CEO of 789 Biz Street, Karen Richardson-Moore has been in business for over 35 years working with entrepreneurs and small business owners and has come to believe certain truths….


  1. Entrepreneurs are a special breed of people. They are driven, creative, proud risk-takers. They believe in their dream.
  2. They believe they can handle most facets of their business by themselves.
  3. They average 12-14 hours a day working on their business and then they put in late hours strategizing to overcome the many challenges of entrepreneurship.
  4. Most times they face these challenges alone experienced expert to advise them…that’s where 789 biz street comes to the rescue!

We pride ourselves in being up late, available when you need that assistance. When everyone else has closed their doors, we’re open all night, just in case you have a question that can’t wait until morning.


789 Biz Street has a full team of experienced, creative, industry-savvy professionals available to provide solutions to problems in the areas of:


  • accounting/bookkeeping
  • web design
  • branding
  • logo and tagline creation
  • social media
  • digital marketing
  • market infusion
  • legal entity formation
  • administrative back-office services
  • insurance
  • licenses/permits
  • tax compliance
