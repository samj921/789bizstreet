Entrepreneur Avenue

Hey, so you’re a new kid on the block. Thanks for stopping by Entrepreneur Avenue. Welcome!

Real talk, here on the Avenue we ask the most important question that every new entrepreneur should be able to answer. What are your business numbers? What do you need to do to break even? What do you need to do to make a profit?

The answer to this 2-part question is the bottom line for anyone in business and if you don’t know the answer every day, then you will not be successful.

Everything you do depends on you knowing your numbers. All the other areas of business are essential but knowing your numbers will help you make strategic decisions regarding each of those other areas.
